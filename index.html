Wrestling with Hard Questions

Regardless of our individual beliefs, all Christians are unified in our belief in God, the LORD and our SAVIOR. We are meant to wrestle with the hard questions, the conflicting perspectives, and the dark times when our faith is shaky. To wrestle with God is not to dishonor Him. To wrestle with God is not to question Him. To wrestle with God is to believe in Him. This devotional site was created for Christians and non-Christians of any faith to address head-on the most controversial Bible and Christianity topics of the 21st century.

What makes this guide different

Deeper than a study Bible note

Translation comparisons

For every key passage, see how the KJV, NIV, ESV, NRSV, NLT, and other major translations render it — and why the differences matter theologically.

Greek & Hebrew word studies

When a single word sits at the heart of a controversy — arsenokoitai, authentein, Gehenna, theopneustos, nephesh, hilastērion — we examine it in its original language in depth.

Textual history & manuscripts

Famous misprints, disputed passages, the Dead Sea Scrolls, and the Comma Johanneum — textual history examined honestly alongside current scholarly consensus.

Archaeological evidence

The Merneptah Stele, the Pilate inscription, the Caiaphas ossuary, ancient Near Eastern flood narratives — physical evidence examined alongside Scripture.
